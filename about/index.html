About

  print("Hello World")

I'm a human that lives in the Bay Area. Professionally, I am the CTO of Manifold, an AI engineering services company. My professional interests include most anything math and computer science related – especially machine learning, signal processing, dynamical systems, Python development, and data intensive software systems built in the cloud. Beyond that my interests are pretty wide ranging – but I'm really into travel, photography, hiking, camping, reading, and being a family guy.

For ten years I hosted my own website, coded from scratch in HTML and CSS, off of the Athena servers back at MIT. For the past ten years, I got caught up in the world of social media and all my content was monetized by other companies. I wanted a little place on the internet of my own again. So I went back to the basics and made this little web site. It's lo-fi, doesn't track anything, and is not personalized to anyone other than me.
